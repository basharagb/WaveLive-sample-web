Article 1: Definitions and Scope

This policy governs the relationship between WaveLive platform and registered hosts (streamers). The following definitions apply throughout this document:

Platform:

WaveLive application, website, and all related services and technologies

Host/Streamer:

Individual who performs live streaming and creates content on the platform

Agent:

Person or entity responsible for managing and supporting hosts

Live Streaming:

Real-time transmission of visual and audio content to audiences

Article 2: Registration Requirements and Eligibility

Age and Identity Requirements

  • • Must be 18 years of age or older
  • • Provide valid government-issued identification for verification
  • • Proof of citizenship or legal residency
  • • No criminal record related to cyber crimes

Technical Requirements

  • • Smart device compatible with WaveLive application
  • • Stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps)
  • • Acceptable quality camera and microphone
  • • Proper lighting for image quality

Skills and Experience

  • • Ability to communicate effectively with audiences
  • • Basic skills in using applications
  • • Commitment to schedules and timelines
  • • Ability to create engaging and appropriate content

Article 3: Host Rights and Obligations

Host Rights

  • ✓ Receive percentage of earnings as agreed
  • ✓ Technical and professional support from agent
  • ✓ Training and professional development
  • ✓ Personal data protection
  • ✓ Participation in activities and competitions
  • ✓ Performance bonuses and rewards

Host Obligations

  • • Adhere to scheduled streaming hours
  • • Create appropriate and engaging content
  • • Respect platform rules and regulations
  • • Positive interaction with audience
  • • Maintain appropriate appearance
  • • No copyright infringement

60%

  • • Payment methods: Bank transfer, digital wallets
  • • Tax and fee deductions per local laws

Article 6: Discipline and Penalties

First Warning

Verbal or written warning for minor violations such as tardiness or quality non-compliance

Temporary Suspension

Streaming suspension for 3-7 days for repeated or moderate violations

Permanent Termination

Permanent termination of cooperation for serious or repeated violations after warnings
