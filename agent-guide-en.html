WaveLive Agent Registration Guide

Complete Guide to Start Your Journey as a Successful Agent

Introduction

Welcome to the world of WaveLive! This guide will help you understand how to register as an agent on the WaveLive platform and start your journey in managing hosts and earning profits through our advanced commission system.

What is WaveLive?
WaveLive is a global live streaming platform that connects agencies and hosts to engage with audiences in real-time. As an agent, you'll get exclusive tools to recruit hosts and manage your team with the potential to earn up to 20% commission.

Registration Steps

1

Download the App

Download the WaveLive app from:

  • Google Play Store for Android
  • App Store for iPhone

Or visit the official website: wave-live.com

2

Create User Account

After downloading the app:

  1. Open the app and select "Create New Account"
  2. Enter required information (name, email, password)
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your profile setup
3

Get Your WaveLive ID

After creating your account:

  1. Go to your profile in the app
  2. Look for "WaveLive ID" or "User ID"
  3. Copy this ID - you'll need it for agent registration
Important: Keep your WaveLive ID safe, you'll need it during the agent registration process.
4

Fill Agent Registration Form

Go to the agent registration page and fill the form with:

  • Your WaveLive ID
  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Additional information as required
5

Account Verification

To verify your account:

  1. Click "Get Verification Code" in the form
  2. You'll receive the verification code in WaveLive app
  3. Enter the code in the form
  4. Click "Submit" to complete registration

Commission System

WaveLive's commission system is based on your team's performance over the last 30 days:

Level Required Points (30 days) Commission Rate
D 0 4%
C 2,000,000 8%
B 10,000,000 12%
A 50,000,000 16%
S 150,000,000 20%

Agent Responsibilities

Host Management

  • Recruit new hosts to join your agency
  • Train hosts on best practices
  • Monitor host performance and provide support
  • Motivate hosts to increase streaming hours

Maintaining Agency Activity

Very Important: You must achieve one of the following conditions every 30 days to maintain your agency activity:
  • Maintain at least 5 active hosts
  • Invite 5 new active agencies
  • Regular coin selling (if you're a coin seller)

Agent Dashboard

After approval of your registration, you'll get access to the agent dashboard which allows you to:

  • Track your agency level and commission rate
  • Monitor earnings and total income
  • Manage hosts and sub-agents
  • Analyze performance and statistics
  • Handle currency transfers and payments

Success Tips

How to Become a Successful Agent?

  • Build Strong Relationships: Communicate regularly with your hosts and provide support
  • Focus on Quality: Choose talented and dedicated hosts
  • Provide Training: Help your hosts improve their skills
  • Monitor Performance: Use the dashboard to track progress
  • Expand Your Network: Invite other agents to increase your earnings

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does registration approval take?

Registration requests are usually reviewed within 24-48 hours from submission date.

Can I change my information after registration?

Yes, you can update most information through the agent dashboard.

How do I receive my commissions?

Commissions are paid weekly through the app's payment system.

Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?

Yes, the minimum withdrawal amount varies depending on the chosen payment method.
