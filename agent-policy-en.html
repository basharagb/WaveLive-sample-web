Agent Policy

Terms and Conditions for WaveLive Agents

Effective Date: October 2025

Important Notice: This document sets forth the terms and conditions governing the relationship between agents and the WaveLive platform. Please read this policy carefully before registering as an agent.

Article 1Definitions

In this policy, the following terms have the meanings specified below:

  • Platform: WaveLive application and website
  • Agent: A person registered to manage hosts on the platform
  • Host: A person who performs live streaming on the platform
  • Agency: A group of hosts affiliated with a specific agent
  • Commission: The percentage of host earnings received by the agent
  • Points: The unit of measurement used to determine agent level

Article 2Registration Requirements

2.1 Eligibility

To register as an agent on WaveLive, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Be of legal age (18 years or older)
  • Have an active account on the WaveLive platform
  • Provide accurate and up-to-date information
  • Agree to all platform terms and conditions

2.2 Registration Process

  1. Complete the registration form with required information
  2. Provide necessary documents for identity verification
  3. Obtain approval from the WaveLive team
  4. Sign the agent agreement

Article 3Agent Rights and Obligations

3.1 Agent Rights

  • Receive commission according to the specified level system
  • Access to the agent dashboard
  • Recruit and manage hosts
  • Invite sub-agents
  • Receive technical support from the WaveLive team

3.2 Agent Obligations

  • Comply with all platform laws and regulations
  • Provide support and guidance to hosts
  • Maintain confidentiality of information
  • Not violate intellectual property rights
  • Act with professionalism and high ethics

Article 4Commission System

4.1 Commission Levels

Commission rates are determined based on total points achieved in the last 30 days:

  • Level D (0 points): 4%
  • Level C (2,000,000 points): 8%
  • Level B (10,000,000 points): 12%
  • Level A (50,000,000 points): 16%
  • Level S (150,000,000 points): 20%

4.2 Commission Calculation

  • Commissions are calculated weekly
  • Commissions are paid within 7 days of week end
  • The platform reserves the right to deduct applicable taxes and fees

Article 5Activity Maintenance Requirements

Mandatory Requirement: Each agent must achieve one of the following conditions every 30 days:
  • Maintain at least 5 active hosts
  • Invite 5 new active agencies
  • Regular coin selling (for agents registered as coin sellers)
Warning: Failure to meet these requirements may result in agency suspension and loss of commission rights.

Article 6Prohibited Conduct

Agents are prohibited from engaging in the following activities:

  • Using illegal methods to increase points or earnings
  • Violating copyright or intellectual property rights
  • Manipulating the commission system
  • Publishing offensive or inappropriate content
  • Circumventing platform rules
  • Using fake or multiple accounts

Article 7Penalties and Sanctions

7.1 Types of Penalties

In case of violation of this policy, the platform may apply the following penalties:

  • Written Warning: For minor violations
  • Temporary Suspension: For 7-30 days
  • Commission Level Reduction: For a specified period
  • Agency Termination: For serious or repeated violations

7.2 Right of Appeal

Agents have the right to appeal any penalty within 15 days of notification.

Article 8Agency Termination

8.1 Voluntary Termination

Agents may terminate their agency at any time with 30 days prior notice.

8.2 Involuntary Termination

WaveLive reserves the right to terminate an agency in the following cases:

  • Violation of platform terms and conditions
  • Failure to maintain activity requirements for two consecutive months
  • Engaging in fraudulent or illegal activities
  • Damaging the platform's reputation

8.3 Post-Termination Obligations

  • Settlement of all financial obligations
  • Return of any materials or information belonging to the platform
  • Cease use of WaveLive trademark

Article 9Data Protection and Privacy

9.1 Privacy Obligations

  • Protect personal data of hosts and users
  • Not share confidential information with third parties
  • Use data only for authorized purposes

9.2 Security

  • Use strong and secure passwords
  • Do not share login credentials
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately

Article 10Policy Amendments

WaveLive reserves the right to modify this policy at any time. Agents will be notified of any significant changes 30 days before they take effect.

Continued use of the platform after amendments constitutes acceptance of the new terms.

Article 11Dispute Resolution

11.1 Mediation

In case of a dispute, it is preferred to resolve it amicably through direct negotiation or mediation.

11.2 Governing Law

This policy is governed by the laws of the country where WaveLive is headquartered.

11.3 Jurisdiction

Local courts have jurisdiction over any legal disputes arising from this policy.

Article 12Contact Information

For inquiries about this policy or any agency-related matters:

  • Email: support@wave-live.com
  • Website: wave-live.com
  • Agent Support: agents@wave-live.com
Acknowledgment: By registering as an agent with WaveLive, I acknowledge that I have read, understood, and agreed to all terms of this policy.
